3d Boxes

Note: Because this sketch uses the OPENGL render, I've only been able to get it to run in Firefox and Chrome. Your experience in other browsers may vary.
Touching or mousing over the top of the screen sketch zooms in, bottom of the sketch zooms out. Left and right rotate, skew, and change the number of boxes.
