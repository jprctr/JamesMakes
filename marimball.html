Marimball

Each column generates a color and tone when the ball collides with it. Mouse over any corner of the canvas to shift the columns in that direction and change the pattern of colors and sound.

Pause is on the right
Reload is on the left

The collision still needs some work, and sometimes the ball will get 'stuck' bouncing between two columns. In this instance it is best to reload.
